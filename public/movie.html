The Best Movies According Tto Me!

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fried Green Tomatoes

Fried Green Tomatoes also has an intelligent and heart-warming story, strong direction and some charming, funny and touching dialogue as well. The ending was suitably uplifting, but I admit I did shed a tear, mostly because I was so impressed by how beautiful the film was. Also superb is the acting.

Twister

Twister, one of the most successful movies of the 90's, mainly due to it's special effects and a lot of people's want for a natural disaster movie that is fun to watch, this was an extremely popular movie.

Marley & Me

About a dog and is a heartwarming romantic comedy that tugs at the audience's emotions with its blend of humor, love, and the inevitable trials of life.
